WOMEN EDUCATION DEVELOPMENT SOCIAL SERVICE TRUST

WOMEN EDUCATION DEVELOPMENT SOCIAL SERVICE TRUST is an organization survives for women and children since 2002 at grass root level. This organization is working for community development and for oppressed people empowerment. This organization is creating the forum for particularly young women leadership in rural areas. So this is the main reason to capacitate those women for their internal empowerment with our legal system. This quarantine period is completely falling down the womens potential on their empowerment process. This project will provide the better opportunity to those women to develop themselves.
